Why YMCA

JC Bose University of Science & Technology takes immense pride in its biggest asset – its students. Our students over many decades have been a flag-bearer of innovation, creativity, commitment, and excellence in the organizations they have gone on to work for and at times even lead. JC Bose UST boasts of very rich alumni..... and we are eager to serve the ever-evolving needs of organizations across different industries and scale by offering the excellent human capital we have nurtured over the years. We take immense pleasure in inviting recruiters and human resource departments of organizations to our campus for extending their internships as well as full-time employment opportunities. Please feel free to reach out to us for any necessary support or queries. The dedicated Training and Placement Office (TPO) at JC Bose UST is fully-equipped to help create the right match between organizations’ unique talent needs and the aspirations borne by our graduating students. We look forward to hear from you!
